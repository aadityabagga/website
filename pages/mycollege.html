The name of my college is
Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology(MSIT)
 It is situated in the capital of India, Delhi
 Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology was established in the year 2001 by the Surajmal Memorial Education Society (1972).This AICTE approved Institute under GGSIP University is ideally located in the Institutional area of Janakpuri, New Delhi. It has a campus spread over eight acres of land with beautiful eco-friendly surroundings.
The Institute aims at achieving the highest standards of academic excellence in the field of engineering and technology, all round development of students as professionals through inventing modes of teaching and research, and educate them to meet the requirements of technologically advanced world.
The institute has a well qualified and experienced faculty. The most contemporary teaching methodology at MSIT provides ample opportunity for students to develop as all round professionals by keeping them abreast of the ever changing technologies.
 It is one of the Leading colleges of GGS Indraprastha University.
It has been certified as an 'A' grade institue by the GGSIP University..
It has produced several gold-medalists and has an excellent track record of placements.
